Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Number Duel is a mobile game developed by Georgios Koutoulas.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may collect, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

Number Duel is designed to collect as little personal information as possible.

The app may store the following information:

  • Player name or display name
  • Game scores and leaderboard results
  • Selected game settings and local progress

If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games, the app may receive your Google Play Games player profile information, such as:

  • Player display name
  • Player ID
  • Profile image, if available

We do not collect your email address, phone number, contacts, messages, or precise location.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to provide and improve the game experience, including:

  • Showing your player name in the game
  • Saving your best scores
  • Displaying leaderboard results
  • Supporting Google Play Games sign-in
  • Improving gameplay and app stability

Local Data

Some data, such as player name, settings, and local leaderboard scores, may be stored directly on your device.

You can remove this data by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

Number Duel may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Games Services, for sign-in and player profile features
  • Supabase, for optional online leaderboard or backend functionality

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Supabase Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

Data may be shared only when necessary to provide game features, comply with legal obligations, or protect the security of the app.

Children

Number Duel is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable law.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review and remove it if necessary.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information used by the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Play without creating a separate email or password account
  • Use or skip Google Play Games sign-in, where available
  • Clear local app data from your device settings
  • Contact us to ask questions about your data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
