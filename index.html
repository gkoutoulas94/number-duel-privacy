Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Number Duel is a mobile game developed by Georgios Koutoulas.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Number Duel may collect, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

Number Duel is designed to collect only the information needed to run the game, online features, leaderboards, ads, and app settings.

The app may collect or store:

  • Player name or display name
  • Google Play Games player profile information, if you choose to sign in with Google Play Games, such as player display name, player ID, and profile image if available
  • Game scores, leaderboard results, difficulty level, number of guesses, and game mode
  • Online match information, such as invite codes, player names, selected difficulty, guesses, match status, and temporary hidden numbers used to run the match
  • App settings, such as sound and volume preferences
  • Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data through Google AdMob
  • Basic device, app, diagnostics, crash, performance, and usage data that may be processed by Google services or other app service providers

We do not collect your email address, phone number, contacts, messages, photos, files, or precise location.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Show your player name in the game
  • Save and display scores and leaderboard rankings
  • Create and join online matches
  • Support Google Play Games sign-in
  • Show ads and measure ad performance
  • Remember app settings
  • Improve gameplay, stability, security, and app performance
  • Respond to support requests

Local Data

Some data, such as player name, sound settings, subscription preview state, and local leaderboard scores, may be stored directly on your device.

You can remove local data by clearing the app data from your device settings or uninstalling the app.

Online Gameplay and Leaderboards

If online features are enabled, Number Duel may store gameplay and leaderboard information in Supabase. This may include player names, scores, invite codes, match status, guesses, and temporary hidden numbers used during online matches.

Online leaderboard and match data may be visible to other players where needed for gameplay, matchmaking, rankings, or joining a room with an invite code.

Ads

Number Duel uses Google AdMob to show ads, including ads after games. Google AdMob may collect and process advertising identifiers, device information, app activity, approximate location, diagnostics, and ad interaction data in order to provide, measure, and improve ads.

You can manage advertising personalization and reset or delete your advertising ID through your Android device settings where available.

Subscriptions and Purchases

Number Duel may show subscription tiers in the app, such as ad-free options. At the time of this policy update, subscription purchases are not connected to live payment processing.

If subscriptions or in-app purchases are enabled in the future, purchases will be handled through Google Play Billing, and we may process purchase status information needed to unlock paid features, restore purchases, and prevent fraud.

Third-Party Services

Number Duel may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Games Services, for sign-in and player profile features
  • Google AdMob, for advertising and ad measurement
  • Google Play Billing, if subscriptions or in-app purchases are enabled in the future
  • Supabase, for online leaderboards and online match backend functionality

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob: https://admob.google.com/

Supabase Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share data only when necessary to provide game features, operate online gameplay, show and measure ads, comply with legal obligations, prevent fraud, or protect the security of the app.

Third-party services listed above may process data as service providers or according to their own terms and privacy policies.

Children

Number Duel is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable law.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review and remove it if necessary.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information used by the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Play without creating a separate email or password account
  • Use or skip Google Play Games sign-in, where available
  • Clear local app data from your device settings
  • Manage ad personalization and advertising ID settings from your Android device settings where available
  • Contact us to ask questions about your data

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app.

Online leaderboard and match data may remain stored while needed to provide the game, maintain rankings, operate online matches, prevent abuse, or comply with legal obligations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
